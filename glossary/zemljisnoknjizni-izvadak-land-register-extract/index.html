What is Zemljišnoknjižni izvadak (Land Register Extract)?

An official certified copy of the Land Registry entries for a specific Croatian property.

Key Facts — Zemljišnoknjižni izvadak (Land Register Extract)
  • Official certified copy of Land Registry entries for a property
  • Shows owner information and all mortgages or encumbrances
  • Issued by the Land Registry office
  • Essential document for property sales and mortgage applications
  • Updated regularly as property interests change

A Zemljišnoknjižni izvadak is an official document from the Croatian Land Registry that shows all information related to a specific property. It provides detailed information about ownership, mortgages, encumbrances, and other interests affecting the property. This document is essential for property transactions and verification of title.

The Croatian Land Registry (Sudski registar nekretnina) maintains comprehensive records of all real property rights and encumbrances. The Zemljišnoknjižni izvadak extracts relevant information for a particular property. The document is regularly updated as property interests change.

Information on the Land Register extract includes the property description, current owner information, and all registered mortgages and encumbrances. The structured format makes it easy to understand the property's legal and financial status. This transparency is fundamental to Croatian property law.

In property transactions, buyers require a current Zemljišnoknjižni izvadak to verify ownership and identify mortgages that must be discharged. Lenders also require this document before approving mortgages. The extract provides the foundation for due diligence in real estate deals.

Obtaining a Zemljišnoknjižni izvadak is straightforward and can be completed through the local Land Registry office. The office issues certified copies that are recognized for all legal purposes. The document is typically available within a few days.

Common Mistake: Using an outdated Zemljišnoknjižni izvadak in property transactions. Always obtain a current extract with the latest information.
Expert Tip: Request the Land Register extract early in the property purchase process. This identifies any issues and allows time to resolve them.

Frequently Asked Questions

Is the Land Registry information public in Croatia?

Yes, the Land Registry is public and anyone can obtain an extract for any property.

How quickly can I obtain a Land Register extract?

Typically within a few days of requesting from the Land Registry office.

How recent should a Land Register extract be for property transactions?

Extract dated within weeks or months is appropriate for transactions.
